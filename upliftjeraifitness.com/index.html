Terms & Conditions
  • Jerai Fitness Upliftment Program can be applied only by the winners of the above state/national/international competitions.
  • Competition Winners from the date Jan 2023 can only apply for this Upliftment Program.
  • Winners of Competitions held before Jan 2023 are not ellibile to apply for the above.
  • The application form submitted will be cross checked thoroughly before the confirmation the Upliftment Program.
  • Any false applications or without proof of winning application will be dis-qualified immediately.
  • Jerai Fitness has the right to qualify or dis-qualify any applicant without any prior notice or information.
  • The Upliftment Program is set for limited applicants for limited time period only. (valid till 30th July 2023)

Points Calculation for Jerai Upliftment Program
  • State Level: 25000 points
  • National Level: 75000 points
  • Int. Level: 150000 points
  • Pro Card: 100000 points
  • Pro Championship:
  • 1st ( Overall) 250000 points
  • 1st Run up: 150000 points
  • 2nd Run up: 100000 points
  • 3rd Run up: 50000 points
  • 4th Run up: 25000 points
Benefits of Points
  • Qualified applicants can convert Points into INR for purchase of any Jerai Fitness Equipment on Retail prices plus taxes.
  • The points will be valid for 1 Year from the date of approval.
  • The points or Upliftment Program cannot be transferred to anyone or converted to cash under any circumstances..
